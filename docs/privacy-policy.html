Privacy Policy

Theme Park IQ  ·  Effective date: June 11, 2026

Thank you for playing Theme Park IQ. This policy explains what information we collect when you use the app, how we use it, and your rights around that data.

Short version: We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any personally identifiable information. The only account data we store is a nickname you choose and your in-game scores.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Theme Park IQ, we collect the following information:

Anonymous Account Identifier

We use Firebase Anonymous Authentication to give you an account without requiring you to register. Firebase automatically assigns a random unique ID to your device session. This ID is not linked to your name, email, or any other personal information.

Nickname

During onboarding you choose a nickname (display name) that appears on the leaderboard. This nickname is visible to other players. We recommend choosing a nickname that does not reveal your real name or other personal details.

Gameplay Data

We record your in-game activity, including:

  • Quiz scores and total score per session
  • Answer streaks
  • Number of correct answers
  • Difficulty level selected
  • Theme park selected
  • Date and time a game was played
  • Number of games played and your all-time best score and streak

Device Storage

We store your authentication session locally on your device (via AsyncStorage) so you stay signed in between app launches. No other data is written to your device outside of normal app operation.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • Name, email address, or phone number
  • Passwords or credentials of any kind
  • Precise or approximate location
  • Contacts or address book
  • Photos, camera, or microphone access
  • Browsing history or cross-app activity
  • Payment or financial information
  • Health or fitness data

3. How We Use Your Information

The data we collect is used solely to operate the app:

  • Display your nickname and scores on the leaderboard
  • Track your personal best scores and streaks across sessions
  • Identify your account so your progress is saved between app launches

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any commercial purpose beyond providing the game experience.

4. Leaderboard & Visibility

Your nickname and daily scores are visible to other authenticated users of the app on the leaderboard. If you do not want your score to be publicly visible, please choose a nickname that does not identify you and be aware that scores are displayed to all players.

5. Third-Party Services

Theme Park IQ uses Google Firebase to power its backend:

  • Firebase Authentication — manages your anonymous account session
  • Cloud Firestore — stores your nickname, scores, and gameplay statistics

Firebase is operated by Google LLC and subject to Google's Privacy Policy, available at policies.google.com/privacy. Data stored in Firebase may be processed on servers located outside your country of residence.

We do not use any advertising SDKs, analytics platforms, or other third-party data processors.

6. Data Retention

Your gameplay data and nickname are retained for as long as your account is active. Because we use anonymous authentication, there is no account deletion flow in the app at this time. If you would like your data removed, please contact us at the address below and we will delete your records manually within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

Theme Park IQ is a general-audience app. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). Because we use anonymous authentication and collect no personal information, the app does not ask for name, email, or contact details from any user regardless of age.

8. Security

All data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS). Access to your Firestore data is restricted by Firebase Security Rules so that only your own anonymous account can read or write your personal records. Leaderboard data is readable by all authenticated users, as described in Section 4.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
